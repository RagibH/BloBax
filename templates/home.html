About BloBax

Healthcare AI with heart

BloBax is built by Md. Ragib Hasan, a Computer Science and Engineering student at Sylhet Engineering College, passionate about real-world AI systems that actually help people.

Inspired by Big Hero 6, BloBax brings compassionate AI to everyday healthcare, ML disease prediction, medicine identification, emergency assistance, and health tracking in one platform.
